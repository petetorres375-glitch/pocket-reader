Pocket Reader

A tiny reader that lives in one file. Open any .epub book or .cbz comic on any phone — no app, no account, works offline. Your place is saved automatically.

Tip: tap the center of the page while reading to hide the bars.

Adding a book

From AO3 (or any site that offers EPUB):

  1. Open the story, tap Download, choose EPUB.
  2. It saves to your phone's Downloads.

Then here in Pocket Reader:

  1. Tap 📚 Library (top bar), then + Add EPUB.
  2. Pick the file from Downloads. It lands on your shelf.

Any .epub book works — not just AO3. Comics work too: open a .cbz file (a zip of page images) the same way. Your place in each is saved automatically.
